Le Male Elixir PARFUM

The voyage is looking torrid with Le Male Elixir, the latest recruit of the Jean Paul Gaultier crew. The flaming torso, his intense perfume for men envelopes itself with sex-appeal to the point of addiction, while the striated gold of his bulging muscles make you melt with desire. Beware of the pull of his skin made copper by the setting sun... Don’t touch! You’ll get burnt! And what of his sailor top? Its translucent brown suggests an amber wake so sensual that a single drop will make you reel.
